ABOUT US

Pizza Delicatus is a one stop pizza joint located in Westlands, Nairobi. Serving hot oven-fresh pizza with its signature all-natural ketchup and using only the freshest ingredients is our commitment. The homemade pizza dough is produced in-house daily and is another reason why the pizza at Pizza Delicatus always tastes better and we have plenty "value-for-money" options that keep our satisfied customers coming back time after time. We value quality and ultimate customer satisfaction.
